Technical Christianity

The Mathematical Defense of the Sign of Jonah

Course Framework

Technical Christianity is an 8-unit, 37-lesson curriculum that moves beyond "faith alone" by applying the same mathematical rigor used in modern physics and information theory to historical claims.

Primary Calculations

1. Bayesian Posterior Probability:

\[ P(R|E) = \frac{P(E|R) \times P(R)}{P(E|R) \times P(R) + P(E|\neg R) \times P(\neg R)} \]

2. The Mechanism Penalty:

P(m|H) ∝ e^(-λk)

Where \( k \) is the number of independent causal mechanisms required for a hypothesis.
