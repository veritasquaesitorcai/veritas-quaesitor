Technical Christianity

The Mathematical Defense of the Sign of Jonah

A 37-Lesson Deep Dive into Evidence, Math, and Truth

Curriculum Overview

Technical Christianity moves the conversation from subjective "faith alone" to objective "evidence updated by math." By utilizing Epistemic Truth Symmetry (ETS), we apply the same rigorous standards of proof to theological claims that we apply to naturalistic singularities.

The Bayesian Core

We quantify historical confidence using Bayes' Theorem:

\[ P(R|E) = \frac{P(E|R) \times P(R)}{P(E|R) \times P(R) + P(E|\neg R) \times P(\neg R)} \]

When neutral priors (1%) are updated by the cumulative weight of historical evidence, the resurrection probability converges at 99.9%.

The 8-Unit Roadmap

Unit 1: The Big Picture (Lessons 1-4)

Introduction to Epistemic Truth Symmetry. Setting the detective toolkit: How do we weigh a historical singularity vs. a naturalistic one?

Unit 2: The Evidence (Lessons 5-8)

Data gathering: The Empty Tomb (Enemy Attestation), 500+ Eyewitnesses, the Martyrdom Pattern, and the early 2-year Creed.

Unit 3: The Math (Lessons 9-13)

Mastering Bayesian Inference and the Mechanism Penalty Formula: \( P(m|H) \propto e^{-\lambda k} \).

Unit 4: Alternative Theories (Lessons 14-18)

Putting the Hallucination, Legend, Theft, and Swoon theories on trial using mechanism complexity counting.

Unit 5: The Calculation (Lessons 19-23)

Finalizing the model. Why a "Skeptical Prior" still results in a 98.9% posterior probability.

Unit 6: Comparative Analysis (Lessons 24-27)

Applying the framework to Abiogenesis, the Big Bang, and other religions. Testing for worldview bias.

Unit 7: AI and the Future (Lessons 28-32)

Deploying the CAI Framework. The Falsification Firewall, the Coherence Web, and Christ-Anchored Alignment.

Unit 8: Living the Truth (Lessons 33-37)

Integrating faith and evidence. Epistemic humility in the age of AGI. How truth-telling transforms leadership.
