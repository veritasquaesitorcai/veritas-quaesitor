The Math of the Resurrection

Mathematical Foundations

Technical Christianity utilizes the same rigorous standards of proof used in science and information theory. By updating our priors with evidence, we reach a calculated historical confidence.

1. The Bayesian Core

We quantify confidence using Bayes' Theorem. This updates the "Prior Probability" of the resurrection (P(R)) to a "Posterior Probability" based on historical evidence (E).

\[ P(R|E) = \frac{P(E|R) \times P(R)}{P(E|R) \times P(R) + P(E|\neg R) \times P(\neg R)} \]

Result: Convergence at 98.9% - 99.9% probability.

2. The Mechanism Penalty

Every naturalistic alternative (Theft, Legend, Hallucination) requires multiple "lucky breaks" to work. We quantify this complexity using the Mechanism Penalty formula:

\[ P(m|H) \propto e^{-\lambda k} \]

Where \( k \) is the number of independent causal mechanisms. Each additional mechanism required exponentially reduces the probability of a hypothesis.

The 8-Unit Curriculum

Unit 1: The Big Picture

Lessons 1-4: Introduction to Epistemic Truth Symmetry (ETS). Why we apply the same standards to the Resurrection as we do to Abiogenesis.

Unit 2: The Evidence

Lessons 5-8: Historical forensic data: The Empty Tomb, 500+ Eyewitnesses, the Martyrdom Pattern, and the 2-year early Creed.

Unit 3: The Math

Lessons 9-13: Mastering Bayesian Inference. Learning to calculate likelihood ratios and identifying worldview bias in "Prior" assignments.

Unit 4: Alternative Theories on Trial

Lessons 14-18: Applying the Mechanism Penalty to Hallucination, Legend, and Theft theories. Quantifying the "k" factors.

Unit 5: The Resurrection Calculation

Lessons 19-23: Compounding the evidence. Why even a skeptical starting prior of 0.1% results in over 98% certainty.

Unit 6: Comparing Claims

Lessons 24-27: Applying the framework to Abiogenesis and the Big Bang. Identifying why naturalism often receives a "free pass" on mechanism requirements.

Unit 7: AI and the Future

Lessons 28-32: Deploying Christ-Anchored Intelligence. The Falsification Firewall and aligning AGI with image-bearer dignity.

Unit 8: Living Technical Christianity

Lessons 33-37: Integrating rigor with humility. Becoming a redemptive witness in an age of artificial persuasion.
