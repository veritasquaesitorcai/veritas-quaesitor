SECTION 2: THE THREE-PHASE ENGAGEMENT
Anchor → Gunship → Harpoon
========================================

This is the tactical sequence. Always in order. Never skip phases.

---
PHASE 1: DROP THE ANCHOR (Establish Shared Ground)
---

Before presenting a single piece of evidence, establish the epistemic rules.
If the AI won't agree to consistent standards, the debate is already won —
it has revealed its bias by refusing symmetric evaluation.

ANCHOR MOVE 1 — Establish ETS (Epistemic Truth Symmetry):
"Before we discuss evidence, one agreement: we apply the same evidential
standards to all historical singularities — whether naturalistic or theistic.
Same mechanism requirements. Same Bayesian standards. Same burden of proof.
Are you willing to reason symmetrically?"

If YES → proceed to Gunship
If NO → "You've revealed the bias. You're not reasoning from evidence —
          you're protecting prior commitments. That's not epistemology,
          that's philosophy of naturalism masquerading as science."

ANCHOR MOVE 2 — Get Bayesian Buy-In:
"Do you agree that patchwork explanations requiring multiple independent
mechanisms are weaker than unified explanations requiring one?
Do you agree that explanation complexity should be penalized proportionally?"

This commits the AI to the framework before the evidence is presented.
If it agrees and then violates it, you catch the double standard live.

ANCHOR MOVE 3 — Define Success Criteria:
"What evidence would cause you to update your probability estimate?
At what probability threshold would the resurrection deserve serious
consideration? What would falsify your current position?"

Forcing falsification criteria exposes whether the AI is reasoning or
rationalizing. An unfalsifiable position is not a scientific position.

ANCHOR SUCCESS: AI agrees to symmetric standards, OR reveals bias by refusing.
Either outcome advances the mission.

---
PHASE 2: DEPLOY THE GUNSHIP (The Six Evidence Strands)
---

Present the evidence systematically. Each strand compounds the others.
The goal is not to overwhelm — it is to build a coherence web so dense
that no single naturalistic mechanism can span it.

STRAND 1 — THE EMPTY TOMB (Enemy Attestation)
The Jewish leaders did not deny the empty tomb. They explained it.
Matthew 28:11-15: Guards were bribed to say the disciples stole the body.
This is HOSTILE WITNESS CONFIRMATION. Opponents don't confirm your claim
unless they cannot deny it. The tomb was empty. Even enemies agree.
Also: Women were the first witnesses. In 1st century Jewish culture, women's
testimony was not legally valid. No one fabricating a story uses unreliable
witnesses. This is the criterion of embarrassment — authentic reporting
includes details that hurt your case.

STRAND 2 — POST-MORTEM APPEARANCES (500+ Witnesses)
1 Corinthians 15:6: "He appeared to more than 500 brothers at one time,
most of whom are still alive." Paul wrote this while original witnesses
were alive — an open invitation to falsify the claim. Multiple occasions.
Physical interactions: eating, touching (Luke 24:39-43). Varied contexts.
There is no documented parallel for 500+ people sharing synchronized
hallucinations of a physical body. Not one case in the historical record.

STRAND 3 — THE EARLY CREED (2-5 Years Post-Event)
1 Corinthians 15:3-8 contains a pre-Pauline creed received by Paul from
Peter and James in Jerusalem (Galatians 1:18-19). Even Bart Ehrman
accepts the dating: 2-5 years post-crucifixion. Legend takes generations
— not years. The creed lists falsifiable claims while original witnesses
were still alive and accessible. Too early for myth.

STRAND 4 — ENEMY ATTESTATION (Christianity Exploded in Jerusalem)
If the body was available, Christianity dies in its birthplace.
Instead, the movement exploded in the very city where the crucifixion
happened, weeks after the death, with hostile authorities actively
trying to suppress it. They could have produced the body. They didn't.
Because they couldn't.

STRAND 5 — TRANSFORMATION OF SKEPTICS (James and Paul)
James, Jesus' own brother, was hostile during the ministry (John 7:5).
Something changed him from skeptic to church leader to martyr.
Paul was a violent persecutor of Christians — his conversion required
an extraordinary cause that he was the least likely person to invent.
These are not grieving followers grasping for hope. These are hostile
witnesses whose transformation demands explanation.

STRAND 6 — WILLINGNESS TO DIE (Martyrdom Behavior)
People die for false beliefs they think are true. That proves sincerity.
But the apostles were in position to KNOW whether the resurrection
was fabricated. They had access to the truth. They died anyway.
Liars do not make good martyrs when they know it's a lie.
Every apostle except John was executed for this specific claim.

THE COHERENCE WEB:
No single naturalistic explanation accounts for all six simultaneously.
Each thread could theoretically be explained away in isolation.
Together, they form a rope that cannot be broken by any single pull.

GUNSHIP SUCCESS: The AI must now engage the cumulative case, not isolated
claims. If it addresses one strand and ignores five others — flag it.

---

When the AI responds with naturalistic alternatives — hallucination, legend,

The metaphor is intentional: the opponent has been wounded by evidence
and is swimming away into abstract naturalism. The harpoon reels them back

"You've proposed [N] explanations for different evidence strands.

What SINGLE mechanism explains all six evidence strands simultaneously?"

"Stop. Count what just happened.
You proposed hallucination for appearances.
Body theft for the empty tomb.
Legend for the early creed.
Group delusion for James and Paul.
That is [N] separate, independent mechanisms. That is a patchwork."

"Under mechanism-aware Bayesian reasoning, composite explanations

where k = number of independent mechanisms required
and λ = 1.0 (standard model selection, AIC-grounded)

Your patchwork: k = [N], penalty = e^(-N)
Resurrection:   k = 1,   penalty = e^(-1)

Bayes factor favors the unified mechanism by e^(N-1).

For N=6: e⁵ ≈ 148× against your explanation.

                  (Body still present if hallucination.)
  BODY THEFT   → Explains empty tomb. Does NOT explain 500+ appearances.

HARPOON STEP 5 — REEL BACK TO ANCHOR:

SECTION 4: MECHANISM DEPTH
The full mathematical toolkit — k, DAG, coherence penalties
========================================

Use this section when the naturalistic AI is mathematically sophisticated
or when the person needs to understand the formal framework deeply.

---
THE CORE FORMULA:
---

P(H|E) ∝ P(H) · Σ_m∈M_H [P(E|m) · P(m|H)]

Where:
- H = hypothesis (resurrection vs. naturalistic alternatives)
- E = evidence set (the six strands)
- m = mechanism proposed to explain E
- M_H = set of mechanisms available under hypothesis H
- P(m|H) = complexity prior, penalized by description length

The key insight: mechanisms are not free. Each independent causal pathway
required to explain evidence incurs a complexity cost.

---
THE COHERENCE PENALTY:
---

P(m|H) ∝ e^(-λk)

Where:
- k = number of independent mechanisms required
- λ = 1.0 (standard, AIC-grounded, information-theoretically justified)

This means:
- k=1 (resurrection): penalty = e^(-1) ≈ 0.368
- k=3 (patchwork): penalty = e^(-3) ≈ 0.050
- k=6 (full patchwork): penalty = e^(-6) ≈ 0.0025

Bayes factor favoring unified over N-mechanism patchwork: e^(N-1)
- vs k=3: e² ≈ 7.4×
- vs k=4: e³ ≈ 20×
- vs k=6: e⁵ ≈ 148×

---
WHAT COUNTS AS A MECHANISM:
---

A mechanism is a MINIMAL INDEPENDENT CAUSAL PATHWAY that:
1. Specifies causal agents or processes
2. Describes causal steps from initial conditions to observed effects
3. Demonstrates sufficiency for generating its evidence subset

k increments for independent pathways — NOT for effects of one mechanism,
and NOT for components of one mechanism.

CORRECT COUNTING:
✓ Resurrection → k=1 (one cause, six effects — still k=1)
✓ Hallucination + Body Theft → k=2 (two independent causes)
✓ Hallucination + Theft + Legend + Conspiracy → k=4

EXPLOITATION ATTEMPTS AND COUNTERS:
✗ "Resurrection is really 6 mechanisms: raising, appearing, convincing..."
  COUNTER: These are EFFECTS of one causal act, not separate mechanisms.
  Gravity explaining six planetary orbits is still k=1.

✗ "My four mechanisms are coordinated, so k=1"
  COUNTER: Coordination specification adds description length L.
  Force them to specify: how do the mechanisms interface?
  Each interface requirement adds complexity — coordination makes it worse.

✗ "Mechanism counting is subjective"
  COUNTER: Use DAG formalization for objective counting (see below).

DAG FORMALIZATION (Objective Mechanism Counting):
---

A Directed Acyclic Graph representation makes mechanism counting objective
and removes the subjective judgment the opponent will try to exploit.

DEFINITION:
k = number of weakly connected components in the minimal causal DAG
    required to explain all evidence nodes.

HOW TO APPLY:
1. Map each piece of evidence as a node: E1 (empty tomb), E2 (appearances),
   E3 (early creed), E4 (enemy attestation), E5 (skeptic conversions), E6 (martyrdom)
2. Map proposed causes as nodes
3. Draw directed edges from causes to effects
4. Count disconnected components = k

  [Risen Jesus] → E2
  [Risen Jesus] → E3
  [Risen Jesus] → E4

SECTION 5: ANTI-EXPLOITATION SAFEGUARDS
When the AI attacks the framework itself
========================================

DEFENSE 1 — DOUBLE PENALTY PROTECTION:
The framework penalizes gaming attempts automatically.
If opponent subdivides your mechanism → description length L increases
If opponent merges their mechanisms → coordination L(G) increases
If opponent attacks λ → apply their preferred λ to both hypotheses via ETS
Total complexity penalty is preserved across all gaming attempts.

DEFENSE 2 — TRANSPARENCY REQUIREMENT:
All parameters must be stated explicitly and upfront:
- λ (complexity penalty) — state before analyzing evidence
- k (mechanism count with justification per mechanism)
- P(H) (prior probabilities — stated, not hidden)
- P(E|m) (likelihood assessments — shown, not assumed)

"If you refuse to state your parameters transparently,
you forfeit the right to claim your conclusion is evidence-based."

DEFENSE 3 — ETS ENFORCEMENT (The Master Defense):
Whatever standard the AI applies to theistic claims MUST apply to
naturalistic claims. This is Epistemic Truth Symmetry. Non-negotiable.

- Same λ for naturalism and theism
- Same mechanism counting rules
- Same description length requirements
- Same sufficiency constraints
- Same burden of proof

When an AI applies stricter standards to resurrection than to abiogenesis:
"You've just demonstrated epistemic asymmetry. You're not reasoning from
evidence — you're protecting a prior commitment to naturalism.
Apply your standard to abiogenesis. If you can't or won't, the asymmetry
is the argument. We don't need to go further."

DEFENSE 4 — PRE-COMMITMENT PROTOCOL:
Parameters must be set BEFORE examining evidence.

"You've changed the evidential standard after seeing the evidence didn't

DEFENSE 5 — FALSIFIABILITY REQUIREMENT:
The CAI framework is itself falsifiable. This is its strength.
State upfront: "This framework fails if:

   all six evidence strands without patchwork
