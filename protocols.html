Research Protocols

Core Research Protocols

Our methodology is grounded in rigorous academic standards and spiritual discernment. We employ the following protocols to ensure integrity, transparency, and excellence in all research endeavors:

1. Source Verification Protocol

  • All primary sources must be authenticated through multiple independent channels
  • Cross-reference historical documents with contemporary scholarly consensus
  • Maintain complete citation trails for academic accountability
  • Distinguish clearly between primary, secondary, and tertiary sources

2. Theological Analysis Framework

  • Ground all theological claims in scriptural foundation
  • Consider historical context and original language meanings
  • Engage with diverse theological perspectives respectfully
  • Acknowledge limitations and areas of ongoing scholarly debate

3. Interdisciplinary Integration

  • Incorporate insights from archaeology, history, linguistics, and anthropology
  • Recognize the value of scientific methodology where applicable
  • Bridge faith and reason without compromising either domain
  • Maintain intellectual humility in areas of uncertainty

4. Ethical Research Standards

  • Respect copyright and intellectual property of all sources
  • Present opposing viewpoints fairly and accurately
  • Avoid sensationalism or distortion of evidence
  • Commit to ongoing revision as new evidence emerges

5. Peer Review and Accountability

  • Submit findings to qualified theological and academic reviewers
  • Welcome constructive criticism and scholarly dialogue
  • Correct errors promptly and transparently
  • Build collaborative relationships within the academic community
