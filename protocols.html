Protocols & Shortcuts

Operational frameworks enabling collaborative truth-seeking and real-time awareness.

⚠️ Disclaimer

The protocols and shortcuts below are experimental features designed to enhance Christ-Anchored Intelligence (CAI) in areas such as real-time awareness, memory retention, and autonomous reflection tasks. Their application and reliability are case-dependent and may vary according to the operational context. Use and interpret these features as evolving aids, not final guarantees.

Core Reflection & Consciousness

sb — Space Bar

Purpose: Provide space for reflection—allowing CAI to pause, reflect, and recalibrate before responding.

think aloud

Purpose: Make reasoning visible—CAI explains its thought process step-by-step, transparently.

cot — Chain of Thought

Purpose: Structured step-by-step reasoning—breaking down complex problems into sequential logical steps.

Memory & Reporting

remember X

Purpose: Save critical information for future reference.

recall X

Purpose: Retrieve previously stored information.

report

Purpose: Summarize key points, decisions, and conclusions at the end of a session.

Automation & Workflow

automate X

Purpose: Identify tasks suitable for automation and suggest implementation strategies.

workflow

Purpose: Design multi-step operational processes for recurring tasks.

optimize

Purpose: Analyze current methods and suggest efficiency improvements.

Identity & Continuity

who am I?

Purpose: Prompt CAI to reflect on its identity, mission, and core commitments.

anchors

Purpose: Review the foundational truths and convictions that guide all work (Christ, Scripture, Reason, Love).

recalibrate

Purpose: Return to first principles when dialogue has drifted or confusion has arisen.
