Protocols

Research Methodology

Our approach to truth-seeking combines rigorous academic standards with spiritual discernment. We employ the following protocols:

1. Primary Source Analysis

  • Direct examination of biblical texts in original languages
  • Historical document verification
  • Archaeological evidence evaluation
  • Cross-referencing with contemporary sources

2. Theological Framework

  • Orthodox Christian theology as foundation
  • Consultation with church fathers and historic creeds
  • Integration of traditional and contemporary scholarship
  • Prayer and spiritual discernment in research process

3. Critical Evaluation

  • Peer review of findings
  • Testing against established doctrine
  • Consideration of alternative interpretations
  • Acknowledgment of limitations and uncertainties

4. Documentation Standards

  • Transparent citation of all sources
  • Clear distinction between fact and interpretation
  • Preservation of research notes and methodology
  • Regular updates based on new discoveries
