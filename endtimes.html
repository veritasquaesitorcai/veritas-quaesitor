CAI End Times Chronology

The Anchor: "Lift Up Your Heads"

Throughout history, believers have been instructed not to be alarmed by wars, earthquakes, and troubles‚Äîthese are "birth pains," but not yet the end (Matthew 24:6-8). However, Jesus gave a specific instruction for recognizing when redemption is truly near:

Luke 21:25-28:
"There will be signs in the sun, moon and stars. On the earth, nations will be in anguish and perplexity at the roaring and tossing of the sea. People will faint from terror, apprehensive of what is coming on the world, for the heavenly bodies will be shaken. At that time they will see the Son of Man coming in a cloud with power and great glory. When these things begin to take place, stand up and lift up your heads, because your redemption is drawing near."

This is the climactic moment‚Äînot the constant state of expectancy throughout the church age, but the specific sign that marks the final approach of Christ's return. The cosmic signs beginning (sun darkened, moon not giving light, stars falling) signal that redemption is imminent. Everything before this is preliminary; everything after this is the fulfillment.

The following chronology is organized around this anchor point: events leading up to "lift your heads" and events following it.

CHRONOLOGICAL SEQUENCE

HISTORICAL EVENTS (Already Fulfilled)

AD 70 - Jerusalem's Destruction:

  1. Jerusalem surrounded by armies, its desolation near - Luke 21:20
  2. Jerusalem falls, people killed and taken prisoner to all nations - Luke 21:24a
  3. Temple destroyed ("not one stone left on another") - Matthew 24:2
  4. Jerusalem trampled by Gentiles begins - Luke 21:24b

Church Age - Israel Dispersed:

  1. Israel experiences hardening in part - Romans 11:25a
  2. Gospel goes to the Gentiles - Acts 13:46, Romans 11:11
  3. Full number of Gentiles being gathered - Romans 11:25a

Modern Era - Israel's Return:

  1. Israel returns to land [1948]
  2. Jerusalem comes back under Jewish control [1967]
  3. "Times of the Gentiles" potentially nearing fulfillment? - Luke 21:24b

PRESENT / NEAR FUTURE - Beginning of Birth Pains

  1. Wars, rumors of wars, nation against nation - Matthew 24:6-7
  2. Famines, earthquakes in various places - Matthew 24:7
  3. False messiahs and false prophets appear - Matthew 24:5, 11
  4. Wickedness increases, love grows cold - Matthew 24:12
  5. Instruction: "Do not be alarmed - the end is NOT yet" - Matthew 24:6

BEFORE 7-YEAR PERIOD (Timing Uncertain)

  1. Third Temple rebuilt? [Not explicitly stated in NT, inferred from 2 Thess 2:4 + Dan 9:27]
  2. Sacrificial system reinstituted? [Inferred from Dan 9:27 "stops sacrifice and offering"]

7-YEAR PERIOD BEGINS

YEAR 0:
  1. Antichrist confirms covenant with many (including Israel?) for "one seven" (7 years) - Daniel 9:27a
FIRST 3.5 YEARS:
  1. Antichrist rises in power/influence
  2. Deception through signs and wonders - 2 Thessalonians 2:9
  3. Two witnesses prophesy in Jerusalem for 1,260 days? - Revelation 11:3
  4. [If temple exists: Sacrifices being offered under covenant arrangement]
MIDPOINT (3.5 years):
  1. Antichrist breaks the covenant (with Israel?) - Daniel 9:27b
  2. Stops sacrifice and offering (in temple) - Daniel 9:27b
  3. Abomination of desolation set up in holy place - Matthew 24:15, Daniel 9:27
  4. Antichrist enters God's temple, proclaims himself to be God - 2 Thessalonians 2:4
  5. "Great Tribulation" begins - Matthew 24:21 ("unequaled from beginning")
  6. Believers in Judea flee to mountains - Matthew 24:16-20
  7. Woman (Israel?) flees to wilderness for 1,260 days - Revelation 12:6, 14
SECOND 3.5 YEARS (42 months / 1,260 days) - GREAT TRIBULATION:
  1. Beast given authority to act for 42 months - Revelation 13:5
  2. Beast blasphemes God, his name, his dwelling - Revelation 13:6
  3. Beast makes war against the saints - Revelation 13:7, Daniel 7:25
  4. False prophet performs signs, deceives inhabitants - Revelation 13:13-14
  5. Image of the beast set up, given power to speak - Revelation 13:14-15
  6. Those who refuse to worship the image are killed - Revelation 13:15
  7. Mark of the beast enforced (right hand or forehead) - Revelation 13:16-18
  8. Cannot buy or sell without the mark - Revelation 13:17
  9. Believers persecuted, hated by all nations - Matthew 24:9
  10. Many turn away from faith, betray one another - Matthew 24:10
  11. Gospel preached to all nations as testimony - Matthew 24:14
  12. Days shortened for sake of the elect - Matthew 24:22

Israel-Specific Tribulation Events:

  1. Two-thirds of those in the land struck down and perish - Zechariah 13:8
  2. One-third left, refined like silver, tested like gold - Zechariah 13:9
  3. This third will call on God's name - Zechariah 13:9

END OF 7 YEARS

  1. "IMMEDIATELY AFTER the tribulation of those days" - Matthew 24:29
  2. COSMIC SIGNS BEGIN:

"LIFT YOUR HEADS" MOMENT

"When these things BEGIN to take place, stand up and lift up your heads, because your redemption is drawing near"

Luke 21:28

AFTER "LIFT YOUR HEADS" (Redemption Sequence)

Israel's Final Battle BEGINS:

  1. All nations gathered to Jerusalem to fight against it - Zechariah 14:2, Zechariah 12:2-3, Revelation 16:14-16
  2. City captured, houses ransacked, women raped - Zechariah 14:2
  3. Half the city goes into exile, half remains - Zechariah 14:2

Divine Intervention (Miraculous):

  1. LORD goes out and fights against those nations - Zechariah 14:3
  2. All nations that attacked Jerusalem destroyed - Zechariah 12:9, Zechariah 14:12, Revelation 19:21
  3. LORD shields those who live in Jerusalem - Zechariah 12:8

Israel's Recognition & Repentance (Immediately After Deliverance):

  1. "Full number of Gentiles has come in" - Romans 11:25b (Gentile age complete)
  2. Spirit of grace and supplication poured out on house of David and inhabitants of Jerusalem - Zechariah 12:10
  3. They look on the one they have pierced (Jesus) - Zechariah 12:10, John 19:37
  4. They mourn for him as for an only child, grieve bitterly - Zechariah 12:10
  5. Fountain opened to cleanse from sin and impurity - Zechariah 13:1
  6. "All Israel will be saved" - Romans 11:26 (Complete Israel = all believing Gentiles from church age + Jewish remnant)

Son of Man's Visible Appearance:

  1. Sign of the Son of Man appears in heaven - Matthew 24:30
  2. All peoples of the earth mourn - Matthew 24:30
  3. Angels sent with loud trumpet call - Matthew 24:31, 1 Thessalonians 4:16
  4. Son of Man comes on clouds with power and great glory - Matthew 24:30, Revelation 1:7
  5. Every eye will see him, even those who pierced him - Revelation 1:7

The Elect Gathered:

  1. The elect gathered from the four winds - Matthew 24:31

Christ Lands on Earth:

  1. His feet stand on the Mount of Olives - Zechariah 14:4
  2. Mount of Olives splits in two (east to west) - Zechariah 14:4

Antichrist/False Prophet Destruction:

  1. Antichrist (beast) destroyed by breath of Christ's mouth - 2 Thessalonians 2:8, Revelation 19:19-21
  2. False prophet captured with the beast - Revelation 19:20
  3. Both thrown alive into lake of fire - Revelation 19:20
