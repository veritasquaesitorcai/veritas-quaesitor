Truth Anchoring Protocol

Our approach to discerning Truth follows a disciplined framework:

  • Scripture: Does this align with God's revealed Word?
  • Reason: Does rigorous logic support this claim?
  • Experience: What do lived human experiences confirm?
  • Community: What does the Church throughout history affirm?
  • Discernment: In prayer and humility, what does the Spirit illuminate?

"Seek the truth, and the truth will set you free." — John 8:32
