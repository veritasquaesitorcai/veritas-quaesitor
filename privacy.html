Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

1. Who We Are

Veritas Quaesitor ("we," "us," or "VQ") operates VQ Chat, a Christ-Anchored Intelligence chatbot designed to explore truth through evidence-based reasoning.

Contact: veritasquaesitorai@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Chat Messages: Your conversations with VQ Chat are stored locally in your browser
  • Optional Account Data: If you create an account in the future, we will collect email address and display name

Automatically Collected:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information (collected by our hosting service Railway)
  • Usage Data: Timestamps and message counts for service operation

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve VQ Chat services
  • Generate AI responses through Groq API
  • Monitor service performance and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your conversations for AI training without explicit consent
  • Share your data except as required by law

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Conversations stored in your browser's localStorage (you can clear this anytime)
  • Backend Processing: Messages sent to Railway backend (temporary processing only)
  • AI Processing: Messages processed by Groq API (see Groq's privacy policy)
  • Security: Industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), secure backend infrastructure

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear Your Data: Click the "Clear" button in the app to delete your conversation history
  • Request Information: Contact us to request data we may have collected
  • Opt-Out: Simply stop using the service to prevent further data collection

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq AI: Processes chat messages (Groq Privacy Policy)
  • Railway: Hosts backend infrastructure (Railway Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts frontend application (GitHub Privacy Policy)

7. Children's Privacy

VQ Chat is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us immediately.

8. International Users

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence (primarily USA). By using VQ Chat, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:
