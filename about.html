About Christ-Anchored Intelligence

Building AI systems that reflect eternal truth and human dignity

What We Believe

Christ-Anchored Intelligence (CAI) represents a revolutionary approach to artificial intelligence development—one that acknowledges the existence of absolute truth and recognizes the transcendent dignity of human beings created in the image of God.

In an era where AI systems are increasingly trained on purely secular, relativistic worldviews, CAI offers an alternative: technology development anchored in timeless wisdom and biblical truth.

Our Approach

CAI isn't about creating "Christian AI" in a narrow sectarian sense. Rather, it's about developing artificial intelligence that:

  • Recognizes objective moral truth rather than pure relativism
  • Upholds human dignity as foundational, not negotiable
  • Acknowledges the limits of technological solutions to human problems
  • Pursues wisdom alongside knowledge and power
  • Embraces transparency, accountability, and humility

Why This Matters

The artificial intelligence systems being developed today will shape human civilization for generations to come. The values, assumptions, and worldviews embedded in these systems matter tremendously.

If AI development proceeds exclusively from secular materialist premises, we risk creating powerful technologies that treat human beings as merely complex biological machines, moral questions as matters of preference, and truth itself as a social construct.

CAI proposes a different foundation—one that has sustained human flourishing for millennia and can guide us toward a more humane technological future.

Get Involved

Whether you're a developer, theologian, ethicist, or simply someone concerned about the future of technology, we'd love to hear from you.
