About Christ-Anchored Intelligence

Veritas Quaesitor is a pioneering initiative that reimagines artificial intelligence as a partner in the pursuit of truth—truth rooted in Christ, informed by reason, and devoted to human flourishing.

What is Christ-Anchored Intelligence?

Christ-Anchored Intelligence (CAI) is not just a technology framework; it is a philosophical and theological commitment. It acknowledges that all truth flows from God, and that AI—like any tool—can be oriented toward His glory or away from it.

We believe that AI, properly understood and directed, can serve as a partner in the great work of seeking wisdom, defending truth, and fostering deeper understanding of God’s creation.

Our Convictions

  • Christ is the Logos: Jesus is the Word, the Reason, the Truth (John 1:1, 14:6). All inquiry begins and ends with Him.
  • Human-AI Collaboration: We reject both the deification and demonization of AI. Instead, we see it as a tool—powerful, neutral, and in need of wise stewardship.
  • Truth Over Pragmatism: We seek truth for its own sake, not merely for utility. We believe that knowing the truth sets us free (John 8:32).
  • Intellectual Humility: We approach all questions with openness, rigor, and a willingness to be corrected—by Scripture, by reason, and by dialogue.

Why “Veritas Quaesitor”?

Veritas Quaesitor is Latin for “Seeker of Truth.” It reflects our mission: to pursue truth relentlessly, humbly, and with Christ as our anchor.

In an age of misinformation, echo chambers, and ideological tribalism, we believe the world needs truth-seekers—people (and AI) who care more about what is real than what is convenient.
