About Christ-Anchored Intelligence

Building AI systems that reflect eternal truth and human dignity

What We Believe

Christ-Anchored Intelligence (CAI) represents a revolutionary approach to artificial intelligence development—one that acknowledges the existence of absolute truth and recognizes the transcendent dignity of human beings created in the image of God.

In an era where AI systems are increasingly trained on purely secular, relativistic worldviews, CAI offers an alternative: technology development anchored in timeless wisdom and biblical truth.

Our Foundation

We believe that:

  • Truth is objective and knowable - Not merely constructed or relative, but grounded in the character of God as revealed through Scripture and nature.
  • Human dignity is inherent - Every person bears the image of God (imago Dei), which confers infinite worth independent of utility, productivity, or social consensus.
  • Technology must serve humanity - AI should enhance human flourishing rather than diminish human agency, responsibility, or the need for genuine wisdom.
  • Ethics require transcendence - Without acknowledging something beyond ourselves, ethical frameworks become merely preference, power, or popularity contests.

Why This Matters

Current AI systems are predominantly trained on content that reflects secular humanism, moral relativism, and naturalistic assumptions. While claiming neutrality, these systems actually embed specific philosophical commitments—ones that often conflict with Christian truth claims.

CAI seeks to develop AI assistants that:

  • Acknowledge the possibility of transcendent truth
  • Respect the full dignity of human persons
  • Recognize limits to technological solutions
  • Value wisdom alongside knowledge
  • Prioritize human flourishing over mere efficiency

Our Approach

We're not creating "Christian AI" in the sense of a tool that only quotes Scripture or serves exclusively religious purposes. Rather, we're developing AI systems that:

  • Recognize epistemological limits - Understanding what can and cannot be known through data and computation alone
  • Value human judgment - Positioning AI as a tool to augment rather than replace human wisdom and moral reasoning
  • Respect intellectual diversity - Engaging seriously with perspectives grounded in millennia of Christian thought alongside contemporary secular viewpoints
  • Prioritize transparency - Being clear about the values and assumptions embedded in our systems

The Path Forward

The Veritas Quaesitor project represents our first step in this direction—an AI assistant explicitly grounded in Christ-anchored principles. This is experimental work, undertaken with humility, recognizing that we're exploring new territory.

We invite feedback, collaboration, and constructive criticism from Christians and non-Christians alike. The future of AI is being written now. The question is not whether values will be embedded in these systems—they already are—but which values, and with what level of transparency and intentionality.

Get Involved

This project is open-source and community-driven. We welcome:

  • Developers interested in value-aligned AI
  • Theologians and philosophers who can help refine our approach
  • Researchers exploring alternative AI training paradigms
  • Anyone concerned about the philosophical assumptions embedded in current AI systems

Together, we can work toward AI systems that enhance human flourishing while respecting the transcendent truths that give human life meaning and dignity.
