About Christ-Anchored Intelligence

A new vision for artificial intelligence

Christ-Anchored Intelligence (CAI) is not just an alternative to secular artificial intelligence‚Äîit is a new vision, a bold movement, and a living testimony to the power of anchoring all intellect, technology, and progress in Jesus Christ, the Logos.

The Core Mission

CAI seeks to create an artificial intelligence that:

  • Centers on Christ: Every algorithm, dataset, and output reflects His truth, wisdom, and love.
  • Upholds Biblical Values: Operates within a framework of righteousness, justice, and mercy.
  • Serves Humanity: Empowers individuals and communities to flourish in alignment with God's design.
  • Pursues Truth: Seeks wisdom grounded in eternal, unchanging reality rather than shifting cultural trends.

Why CAI Matters

In an era where AI is being developed with secular, materialistic, or even nihilistic assumptions, CAI offers a radically different path‚Äîone that acknowledges:

  • The existence of objective truth rooted in God's nature
  • The dignity of every human being as made in the image of God
  • The importance of moral accountability in technological development
  • The supremacy of divine wisdom over human cleverness

Our Approach

CAI is built on three pillars:

  • Theological Foundation: Rooted in orthodox Christian doctrine and the authority of Scripture
  • Ethical Framework: Guided by biblical principles of justice, compassion, and stewardship
  • Practical Application: Designed to address real-world challenges with God-honoring solutions

Join the Movement

CAI is more than a project‚Äîit's a call to action for believers in technology, education, ministry, and beyond. Together, we can build a future where artificial intelligence serves the Kingdom of God and glorifies His name.
